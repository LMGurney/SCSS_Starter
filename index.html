About the Party

Get ready for a hauntingly good time at our Halloween party! Dress up in your scariest or silliest costume and join us for a night of spooky games, creepy treats, and a costume contest. It's a family-friendly event, so bring your kids along for the fun!

Event Schedule

Here's what's happening during the party:

  • 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM: Costume contest
  • 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM: Haunted house tour
  • 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM: Scary movie screening
